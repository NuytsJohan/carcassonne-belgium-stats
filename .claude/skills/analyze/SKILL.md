name: analyze
description: Deep analysis of a question, problem, or decision — break it down, consider trade-offs, and provide a structured recommendation
---

Analyze the given question or problem thoroughly. Follow this structure:

## 1. Understand the problem
- Restate the core question or problem in your own words
- Identify any ambiguities or assumptions

## 2. Break it down
- Decompose into smaller sub-questions or components
- Identify what information is needed to answer each part

## 3. Research
- Search the codebase, data, or external sources as needed
- Gather relevant context and evidence

## 4. Evaluate options
- List possible approaches or answers
- For each, consider pros, cons, and trade-offs
- Flag risks or unknowns

## 5. Recommendation
- Provide a clear, actionable recommendation
- Explain the reasoning behind it
- Suggest next steps if applicable

Be thorough but concise. Use evidence from the codebase and data where relevant. If the problem is unclear, ask clarifying questions before diving in.
